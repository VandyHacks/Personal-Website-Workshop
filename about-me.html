Conner Pinson (Vanderbilt University, Class of 2020) is a Computer Science student on the Financial Engineering track. His areas of interest include machine learning and computational economics. He helps organize the annual hack-a-thon VandyHacks at Vanderbilt as a member of the communications committee. He has also worked on the Region Radio project with professor Douglas Fisher. He runs a YouTube channel focusing on commentating on current events, specifically on the Internet and video game industry. He has been involved with multiple theater organizations on campus (Vanderbilt Off-Broadway and The Original Cast) as a performer, executive board member, and artistic board member.
